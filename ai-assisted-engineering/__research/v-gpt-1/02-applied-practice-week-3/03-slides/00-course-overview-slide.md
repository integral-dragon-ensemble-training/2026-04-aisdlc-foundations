# Course Overview Slide

## Slide 1 — AI-SDLC Overview: Use AI to Pay Down Friction, Not Just Produce More Code

**Core message:** The course is about using AI to improve changeability, confidence, and engineering flow.

**Bullets**
- AI is most useful when it helps teams understand and improve existing systems.
- Technical debt is anything that raises the cost, time, or risk of change.
- The five-step method is: define good, identify debt, use AI, validate, repeat.
- The course treats tests, observability, security, and release discipline as part of maintainability.

**Suggested visual**
- A left-to-right flow: Good project health → Friction / debt → AI-assisted inspection & cleanup → Validation → Repeatable practice

**Speaker notes**
Open the course with this slide. It should establish that the week is not about prompt tricks or feature generation theater. It is about better engineering through AI-assisted visibility and cleanup.
