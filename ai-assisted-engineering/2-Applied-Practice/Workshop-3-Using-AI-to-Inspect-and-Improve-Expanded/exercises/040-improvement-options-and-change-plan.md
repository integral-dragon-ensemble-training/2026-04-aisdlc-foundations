# 040 Improvement Options And Change Plan

## Objective

Turn findings into small improvement options.

## Claude Prompt

```text
Based on the evidence-backed findings, propose three improvement options.

For each option, include:
- what changes
- files likely touched
- expected benefit
- risk
- validation method
- why this is small enough for review

Recommend one option and produce a change plan.
Do not edit files yet.
```

## Output

- three improvement options
- selected change plan
- validation plan
