# Workshop 5 Expanded Narrative

Workshop 5 is where Applied Practice becomes a team habit.

The prior workshops produced evidence: scorecards, friction maps, AI inspection findings, candidate branches, validation decisions, and rescore deltas. Workshop 5 asks what the team should actually do with that evidence.

The instructor should resist rewrite language. The right question is not "how do we fix everything?" The right question is "what cadence lets us reduce friction every week without destabilizing delivery?"

Participants synthesize their individual work, compare branches, and decide what should be merged, revised, backlogged, or discarded. Then they create a 30-day plan and working agreements for AI-assisted engineering.

By the end, the team should have a lightweight operating model: how they pick AI-assisted improvement work, how they branch, how they validate, how they review together, and how they measure whether the repo is getting easier to change.
