# 040 ADO Board And Cadence

## Objective

Map the improvement model into ADO board usage and team cadence.

## Claude Prompt

```text
Turn the 30-day improvement plan into an ADO board/cadence proposal.

Include:
- suggested work item titles
- acceptance criteria
- validation evidence
- review owner
- cadence: weekly, biweekly, or sprint-based
- how scorecards and friction maps are updated
- how group review decisions are captured

Do not assume manager approval. Mark items that need approval.
```

## Output

- ADO work item draft list
- cadence proposal
- approval dependencies
