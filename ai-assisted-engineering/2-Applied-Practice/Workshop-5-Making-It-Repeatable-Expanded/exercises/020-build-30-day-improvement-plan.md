# 020 Build 30-Day Improvement Plan

## Objective

Turn evidence into a realistic 30-day team plan.

## Claude Prompt

```text
Build a 30-day improvement plan from the Applied Practice evidence.

Include:
- three quick wins
- two structural improvements
- one workflow improvement
- one validation improvement
- owner or role
- expected benefit
- validation method
- ADO work item suggestion

Avoid rewrite plans. Keep work small enough to review.
```

## Output

- 30-day improvement plan
- ADO work item candidates
