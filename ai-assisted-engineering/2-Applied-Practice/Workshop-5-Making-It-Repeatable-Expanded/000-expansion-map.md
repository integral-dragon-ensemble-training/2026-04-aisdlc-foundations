# 000 Expansion Map

Workshop 5 turns the prior workshop artifacts into a team practice.

## Expanded Flow

```text
scorecards
  + friction maps
  + AI inspection packets
  + validation decisions
  -> 30-day plan
  -> team AI working agreements
  -> ADO board/cadence
  -> group merge decisions
  -> repeatable operating model
```

## Core Idea

Do not create a rewrite fantasy. Create a working cadence for small improvements.

## Outputs

- synthesis of Workshops 1-4
- 30-day improvement plan
- team working agreements for AI-assisted development
- ADO board/cadence proposal
- group merge/backlog decision log
- repeatable operating model

## Guardrails

- Avoid grand modernization promises.
- Keep improvements small and reviewable.
- Preserve validation discipline.
- Make ownership explicit.
- Do not treat AI output as automatically mergeable.
