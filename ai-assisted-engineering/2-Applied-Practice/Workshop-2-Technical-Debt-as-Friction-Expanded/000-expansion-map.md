# 000 Expansion Map

Workshop 2 expands the scorecard from Workshop 1 into a friction map.

## Input From Workshop 1

- baseline repository health scorecard
- weak project-health categories
- architecture/setup/testing findings
- participant branch and coursework workspace

## Expanded Flow

```text
Workshop 1 scorecard
  -> friction inventory
  -> debt taxonomy
  -> interest-rate estimate
  -> flow-impact prioritization
  -> one candidate debt-reduction change
  -> group friction review
```

## Core Idea

Debt should be described as observable friction:

- who pays the cost
- how often they pay it
- what change it slows
- what evidence shows the friction
- what small improvement would reduce future interest

## Outputs

- friction register
- debt item decision records
- ranked top debt items
- candidate improvement branch summary
- group backlog recommendations

## Guardrails

- Do not turn the exercise into a complaint list.
- Require repo evidence for every debt claim.
- Prefer repeated friction over one-time annoyance.
- Avoid broad rewrites.
- Keep candidate changes small enough for group review.
