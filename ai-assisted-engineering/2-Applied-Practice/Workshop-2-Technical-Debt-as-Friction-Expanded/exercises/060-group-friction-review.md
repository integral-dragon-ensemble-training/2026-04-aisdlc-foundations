# 060 Group Friction Review

## Objective

Compare participant friction maps and decide what should become team backlog work.

## Claude Prompt

```text
Prepare my Workshop 2 group review packet.

Include:
- target repo
- branch name
- top friction items
- evidence behind each item
- interest estimate
- selected candidate improvement
- validation result
- recommendation: merge, revise, backlog, or discard
- open questions for the team

Write the packet to 060-group-review/group-review-packet.md in my coursework folder.
```

## Output

- group review packet
- backlog candidates
- merge/revise/discard recommendation
