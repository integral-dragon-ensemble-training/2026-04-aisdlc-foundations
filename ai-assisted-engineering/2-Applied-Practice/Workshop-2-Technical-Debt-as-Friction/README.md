# Workshop 2 — Technical Debt as Friction

Workshop 2 of the AI-SDLC Applied Practice course. This workshop reframes technical debt as the repeated tax a system charges on every change — broader than messy code, and measurable in terms of who pays interest, how often, and on what. Participants leave with a ranked, categorized friction map of a real repo or workflow, not a vague complaint list. The workshop covers the nine-category debt taxonomy, the principal-vs-interest metaphor, and a priority lens based on impact on flow vs. cost to fix.
