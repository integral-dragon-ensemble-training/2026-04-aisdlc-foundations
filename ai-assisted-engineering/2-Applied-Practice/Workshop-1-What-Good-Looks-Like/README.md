# Workshop 1 — What Good Looks Like

**Module 02 (Applied Practice) · Workshop 01**

Workshop 1 sets the quality target for the course. Participants learn why AI-assisted engineering needs a picture of "healthy" before it can diagnose debt or propose improvements. The nine-category project-health framework and the top-12 checklist give them a concrete rubric they can apply to any repo in under 20 minutes. The workshop ends with a Repository Health Scorecard exercise against a real public repo so participants leave with an inspectable artifact, not abstract agreement.
