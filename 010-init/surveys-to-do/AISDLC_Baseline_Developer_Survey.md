# AISDLC Baseline Developer Survey (Week 0 / Baseline)

**Purpose:** Help us tailor training, remove friction, and measure progress over time (Baseline → Week 4 → Week 8).  
**Time:** 8–12 minutes.  
**Privacy:** Results will be shared in aggregate. Free-text responses may be summarized; avoid including sensitive project/customer data.

## Response Scales Used
- **Likert (Agreement):** 1 = Strongly disagree, 2 = Disagree, 3 = Neutral, 4 = Agree, 5 = Strongly agree
- **Confidence:** 1 = Not confident, 2 = Slightly, 3 = Moderately, 4 = Very, 5 = Extremely
- **Frequency:** Never / Monthly / Weekly / Several times per week / Daily
